About the Game

Immerse yourself in a dynamic 3D world where your every move matters. Guide a rolling ball through procedurally generated landscapes, shaped by color-based seeds that bring each playthrough to life in a unique way. The world is constantly changing, offering fresh challenges and surprises.

Key Features:

  • Endless worlds, each uniquely generated using color sequences
  • Interactive environment with changing blocks and strategic gameplay
  • Challenging AI-controlled balls that hunt you down
  • Intense local multiplayer battles via Bluetooth
  • Multiple levels, each ramping up the difficulty

Gameplay Highlights

  • Unlockable worlds with increasing complexity
  • Survive against AI adversaries, each with their own tactics
  • Manipulate the world through color interactions
  • Fast-paced multiplayer action with Bluetooth support for local play
  • Challenge yourself with progressive levels full of unexpected twists

Behind the Game

Crafted by Bjarnik Interactive, a small yet passionate team committed to creating fresh and exciting gaming experiences. Built using Unity, this game features cutting-edge AI, procedural world generation, and intense multiplayer mechanics to keep you on your toes.
